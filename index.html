About Me

My name is Savannah Andrzejewski. I am a Metro-Detroit based photographer who is always willing to travel! I find every shoot to be an adventure because I love getting to know people, exploring different locations, and being able to use my camera to create.

My goal as your photographer is to make you feel comfortable and confident. You are more than welcome to listen to music or bring a loved one to hype you up during your shoot! I can't wait to meet you and use my camera to freeze moments in time, providing you with pictures beyond words.
